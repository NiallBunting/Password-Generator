DDPG - Data Derived Password Generator


Passwords based on data you input.

Not all sites support the type of passwords that the DDPG generates. Below is a list of sites that do support it. Feel free to email me any other sites that support what the DDPG generates.

It generates passwords that are:

  • 16 characters in length.
  • All alphanumeric characters, both uppercase and lowercase (a-z, A-Z and 0-9).
  • Contains the symbols: '/', '+' and '='.
